Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Plate AI ("Plate AI," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website, applications, and services (the "Service"). By using the Service, you agree to the practices described here.

  • Account & contact information — such as your email address when you join our waitlist or create an account.

  • To analyze usage, maintain security, prevent fraud and abuse, and improve our AI models and features.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

Plate AI uses artificial intelligence to analyze photos and other inputs you provide in order to identify ingredients and generate recipes and cooking guidance. To do this, your content (such as images and text) may be processed by our systems and by trusted third-party AI providers that help power these features. We may use de-identified or aggregated data to develop and improve our models and Service.
